Multi-Lingual Templates
 
As a part of Tekion's global expansion, our platform has to be ready to support many languages. We also need to enable our dealer partners who have diverse clientele. Our communication templates now can be set up and shared in multiple languages.

Transactions Tab Revamp in Lead details
 
Viewing independent transactions across sales/service/parts does not help salespeople draw patterns from history and pitch better for the future. With this new approach—'Ownership History', the transactions are grouped by vehicles. This will enable salespeople to get insights about leads' vehicle interests and service frequency, which would lead to richer business opportunities.
