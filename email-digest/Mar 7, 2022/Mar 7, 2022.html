Top 3 Figma Plugins!
 
Sometimes, Designing beautiful experiences is hard. But it doesn’t have to be! Here are top 3 Figma plugins to help you get through your busy week!
 
 
 
#1 Design Lint
 
Design Lint is a plugin that finds missing styles in your design! One of the best part about this plugin is that there's no logging in, accounts, or syncing needed! How cool! While it's running, Design Lint will update automatically as you fix errors one by one!

#2 Autoflow
 
Autoflow makes it easy to draw flows. Simply select 2 objects and a line will be magically drawn between them. This tool is super useful when explaining your ideas to a product manager or even during Dev Hand-off!

#3 Chart
 
The chart is a plugin for Figma that uses real, random data to create the most popular charts! Chart supports copy-paste from editors like Excel, Numbers, Google Sheets and remote JSON (REST API), local CSV, and JSON files.
