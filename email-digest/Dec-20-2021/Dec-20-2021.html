YouTube Design Channels Worth Watching
 
YouTube is slowly becoming (already become) the new TV – and people are relying more and more on it to get updates on the topics they care about. Here are some popular design channels for you to follow.
 
 
 
 
▶️ femke.design
 
Hi! I'm Femke, a product designer in Toronto, Canada working in tech at Uber. On this channel i'll be sharing my learnings and experience being a designer, which will cover everything from UX Design to User Research, Prototyping and Product.
 
 
 
 
▶️ Punit Chawla
 
I'm a professional UX/UI Designer from India. I post awesome videos every monday and Thursday on everything design. Subscribe to my channel for videos on Web design, UI design, Adobe Xd Tutorials, Figma Tutorials, Design News & more!
 
 
 
 
▶️ AJ&Smart
 
Hey, we're AJ&Smart! We're a product design and innovation studio based in Berlin, Germany! We work with some of the biggest companies in the world to help them build better products faster. It's our goal to give you as much value as possible here on our Youtube channel.
 
 
 
 
▶️ Flux
 
Flux is the personal vlog of Ran Segall, a designer and entrepreneur. On the show I'm sharing my journey, struggles and lessons I learn as I build my career as a designer and a business owner.
 
 
 
 
▶️ Mizko
 
I’m a 30 year old designer & entrepreneur. As of Jan 2021, I have taken a step away from the agency land to focus on education and sharing my experiences with the next generation of designers that want to change the world.
