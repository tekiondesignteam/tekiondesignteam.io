CRM Mobile App
 
Salesperson’s goal is to interact with leads, keep them engaged, and establish a lasting relationship.

Our mobile experience imitates our desktop CRM, and allows the user to access the leads data and enable engagement from anywhere, at any time right at their finger-tips.

Rule Engine
 
Businesses define rules to run seamless operations and effortlessly take right decisions. Rule Engine helps set up, automate these rules. With Rule Engine, business processes get streamlined, reduce human errors and increase productivity.

Tekion’s Rule Engine design is inspired by the Business process management softwares like Kissflow, ProcessMaker & ZohoFlow. This experience will enable dealers to define both simple to complex rules.

Rockstars behind the Project:
Mani Bharathi Ravi & Varun Kumar S
