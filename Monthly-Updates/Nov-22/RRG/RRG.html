PARTS
 
 
Warehouse Setup
Figma Design Link
 
 
Warehouse Management is supplementary ARC application under “Parts Settings” that enables users to view, create, edit, and archive warehouses based on roles (access). Separate inventory for every warehouse will optimise business operations, one level below the site. Most of the business operations are performed at a warehouse level except few like stock valuation.
 
Shaan Shivanandan
Product Design Lead
 
 
 
Core Inventory Management
Figma Design Link
 
 
Core parts are managed as a separated individual part numbers in Renault. All the details and values of the core part numbers will be provided in Renault central part master. Central management of core part number will avoid multiple manual manipulations across different part numbers. All the changes can be done in one screen and will be applied everywhere. Easier usage of all specific core parts data for reports, price calculation.
 
Vidhisha Kedia
Senior Product Design
