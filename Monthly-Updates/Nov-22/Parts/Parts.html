RRG: Core Inventory Management
Figma Design Link
 
 
Core parts are managed as a separated individual part numbers in Renault. All the details and values of the core part numbers will be provided in Renault central part master. Central management of core part number will avoid multiple manual manipulations across different part numbers. All the changes can be done in one screen and will be applied everywhere. Easier usage of all specific core parts data for reports, price calculation.
 
Vidhisha Kedia
Senior Product Design
 
 
 
Prepaid Flow for SO and RO
Figma Design Link
 
 
With the new ability to execute negative sale and deprecation of shortage sale flow, users need the ability to mark parts prepaid by customers. This new flow will address some of the existing shortcomings and work arounds of prepaid flow using shortage sale. The changes are going to be for both Sales Order and Part RO sales. There is improved visibility of prepaid parts on the SOR flow as well. There will be a phased rollout of additional capabilities to the prepaid flow.

Senior Product Design
