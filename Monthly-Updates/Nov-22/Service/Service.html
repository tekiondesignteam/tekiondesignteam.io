Fee Management
Figma Prototype
 
 
RRG has 2 types of fees, first one is Waste Management Fee also called as Eco participation fee or ECO Tax and the second one is Warranty Management Fee.Waste Management fee is the amount applied to invoice for waste/recycle management dealership carries out. This fee is only applied on Repair order for customers that are cash paying, term paying or Insurance. Additionally this fee is only applicable if there is a Mechanical or Body shop related activity on RO.
