OCR Invoice Automation
Figma Prototype
 
 
In the current scenario, the invoice information has to be fed manually to the system. To reduce the manual effort of the user in the accounting department, we are incorporating OCR technology and automating the information-feeding process. One of the many challenges is that the OCR is not 100% accurate in reading and processing the Data. Through this design, we have solved the problems that may occur when processing the files and making necessary edits by the user to make the process more seamless.

Tekion Wallet - Business cards activation
Figma Prototype
 
 
The business cards are issued to individual employees or the whole company with custom spending limits and spend categories to allocate funds and track the dealership's expenses.
Features:
  • Controllers can create a new Business card for the employees or company and edit the spending limit and spend categories frequently.
  • Physical cards can be shipped to the employees’ registered shipping address (modifiable).
  • The employee must enter the card credentials to activate and start using the card.

Transaction limit & Surcharge Fee
Figma Prototype
 
 
Transaction limit: Due to fraud and other reasons, the Dealership would want the flexibility to set transaction limits to certain payment modes. In the Tekion pay settings, the Tekion pay Admin will be able to set the transaction limit to the respective payment mode.

Surcharge Fee: Going forward, dealerships will have the control and ownership of turning the surcharge fee flag on or off based on their state rules and policy. They will be liable for turning the flag on if their state doesn’t permit passing a surcharge fee to customers. The Dealership will receive a disclaimer on the same when they turn on the Surcharge fee flag.

Tekion Pay - Disputes revamp
Figma Prototype
 
 
A dispute (also known as a chargeback) occurs when a customer questions the dealership about their payment. The arguments have to be responded to within certain days. Failing to respond may result in losing a lot of money. Disputes can be filed by submitting the reasons, supporting evidence, and receipts.
Problem Statement:
  • Response time for critical disputes is not evident and is mixed up with other disputes in the list.
  • Lack of knowledge about the relevant evidence to be submitted. Currently, we are listing all the possible proof to be submitted, which is confusing.
Solution:
  • Right, Nudge at the right time: Now the users will first land on the pending response tab, which will filter the critical disputes which need a response and clearly shows the time left to submit evidence.
  • Recommendations: Now, users get recommendations on relevant evidence needed for respective disputes and best practices to win disputes.
