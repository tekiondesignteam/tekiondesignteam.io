Chat Platform - August Updates
 
 
 
Live Chat - Mobile App
 
Sneha A | Figma Prototype
 
 
Agents are not in-front of the system all the time. Sometimes they need to continue the chat with the customer through their phone. Agents should have capability to accept the chat via mobile and continue to do basic actions like lead creation, tagging and chatting. Live chat on mobile helps the agents to be connect with the customers on the go.
 
 
 
Contact Centre: Agent Console
 
Sneha A, Manali More | Figma Prototype
 
 
Enhancements to our current chat console: Dealerships do not often have enough staff to support live chat. Even if staffs are available, they might not be available to respond to at all times. Building a similar call center approach which allows dealers to outsource live chat will increase dealer adoption of live chat.
