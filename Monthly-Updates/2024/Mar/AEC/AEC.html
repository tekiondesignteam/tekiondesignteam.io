Standalone Appointment Flow
Figma File
Currently, appointment scheduling is limited to the VDP page of a specific vehicle. However, customers often choose a vehicle before reaching this point. Therefore, a standalone appointment page is required to improve visibility, generate leads, and increase appointment numbers, ultimately boosting revenue.
Flow: Users select vehicle model, dealership, date, and time, provide contact details, and receive a confirmation email with an option to reschedule.

Integration of Data Mapping in Acura ZDX Project
Figma File
This month, we achieved a significant milestone by seamlessly integrating data from all Airtable databases into the flows designed for Acura ZDX, enhancing flexibility and scalability in future design iterations. Despite encountering multiple challenges during the mapping process, we effectively resolved them through collaborative and problem-solving discussions within the team.
