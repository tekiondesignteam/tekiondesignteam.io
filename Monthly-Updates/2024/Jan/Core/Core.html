Central Employee Onboarding
Figma Prototype
Central Employee Onboarding streamlines the entire onboarding process by merging employee onboarding and user setup into a unified "Create Employee" section. Users can effortlessly input general information, user access, employment details, payroll, bank info, and tax details. It aims to eliminate an independent workflow for linking employee to user, providing a comprehensive onboarding solution in one go.
