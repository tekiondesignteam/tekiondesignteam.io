Reserve Tech in RO
Figma Link
At Dealerships like Shaganappi and BMW Gallery transitioning from R&R, CDK Shop work distribution, Service Dispatcher needs to be able to force an RO to a tech to be his/her next RO to work on, whether the DP follows manual dispatch or has signed up for auto dispatch. By putting it in reserve status, once a tech finished their current RO or RO/Job(s), the system would then automatically assign them the reserve RO and and Techs can identify a reserved RO that got assigned to them and will be able to pick it up and work on it as high priority.

RRG - Incentive Planning and Bonus Calculation
Figma Link
Service Managers, Service Directors or Controllers at a dealership need a way to calculate the productivity of their technicians w.r.t their objectives to determine which technicians qualify to be paid productivity bonuses. They need a way to calculate the productivity bonus that needs to be paid to their Technicians. We are solving the problem, by introducing this approach.
