IoT - Vehicle Tracking Management
Figma Prototype
Vehicle tracking refers to the process of monitoring and locating vehicles in real time. It involves the use of hardware and software to track the location, movements, speed, and sometimes other vital parameters of vehicles, such as fuel consumption or engine status.
Vehicle Tracking Management application in TAP is designed for Tekion employees.
The main objectives would be to manage Vehicle Tracking Hardware to System by adding devices, provisioning devices to dealers, configuring devices and locations, archive devices etc., and manage maps for individual Sites of the dealers by creating maps and adding location details, updating the map, etc.
