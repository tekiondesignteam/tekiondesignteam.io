AP/AR Adjustments

This enhancement enable dealer partners to efficiently reclassify/adjust transactions between Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable, streamlining the management of consolidated positions.
For example, in OEM collaborations, where both receivables and payables are managed, this feature ensures swift handling of net payouts, amplifying accounting agility for streamlined financial operations.
