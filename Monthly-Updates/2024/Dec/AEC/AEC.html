ZDX Components & Variables
Figma File
We've enhanced scalability and consistency for graceful degradation screens by developing components and applying variables. Specifically, implementing a global visibility variable for error state in modal boxes (e.g., location) was a successful approach, allowing it to adapt from desktop models to mobile bottom sheets. We designed this solution to ensure the screen scales uniformly across all resolutions, including changes in behaviour tailored for mobile and touch devices.

Co-buyer My Order Cards
Figma File
When a buyer adds a co-buyer to the deal, a link will be sent to the co-buyer to fill in their details in the credit application and upload and sign the necessary documents. A' My Order' section has been incorporated to inform the co-buyer of all these steps, displaying order status and other relevant details.
