User Setup
Figma Prototype
Our focus has been on enhancing user experience and flexibility within the platform. We have successfully implemented a dynamic user setup feature that allows individuals to create both corporate and non-corporate accounts based on their role type within the organisation. One of the key advancements is the empowerment of users to tailor their product access. Depending on their role and region, users can now customise their access to specific features, ensuring a more personalised and efficient experience within our enterprise product.
This development not only streamlines user interactions but also aligns with our commitment to providing a more intuitive and adaptable platform for our diverse user base.

Sequence Setup - Document & Invoice
Figma Prototype
The Sequence Setup feature plays a crucial role in defining document and invoice sequences along with their patterns generated during diverse dealership processes. Our recent design update enhances this functionality to accommodate advanced use cases, specifically tailored for North America, Inchcape, and RRG dealerships. Enterprise Roles and Permission
Figma Prototype
The Roles and Permissions module in ARC has been redesigned to address the specific needs of enterprise-level applications, aligning with Inchcape's requirements. This enhanced functionality now allows corporate or enterprise users to centrally configure roles and associated permissions within the corporate workspace. Approval of role requests is streamlined through a centralized workspace, providing efficient oversight. Additionally, user management for various dealership and subsidiaries is now conveniently handled within a unified workspace.
