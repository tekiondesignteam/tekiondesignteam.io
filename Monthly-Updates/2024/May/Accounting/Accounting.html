Figma Link
With this enhancement, we are optimizing ARC accounting processes with a new enterprise layer for large-scale dealerships including Inchcape and Asbury.

Positive Pay Automation
Figma Link
Now, dealer users can effortlessly automate and schedule their positive pay file transfers to banks, streamlining the process and reducing the risk of delays. Positive pay files are essential for verifying checks and preventing fraud by the details of issued checks with those presented for payment. Initially launching for Asbury, this feature will soon expand to all US dealers.
