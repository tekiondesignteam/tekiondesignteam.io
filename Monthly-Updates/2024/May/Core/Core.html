Add Customer Standardisation - Customer Duplicate Prevention
Figma Prototype
Customer Standardization & Duplicate Prevention system ensures consistency across all customer touchpoints and prevents duplicate customer records within the ARC system. It maintains data integrity by implementing validation checks during customer creation, identifying potential duplicates, and offering options to select existing records or create new ones. This streamlines data entry and reduces redundancy.
