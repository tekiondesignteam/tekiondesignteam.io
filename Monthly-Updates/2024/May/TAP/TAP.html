PayTek - Payment Settings
Figma Prototype
Payments on ARC was managed through two different settings if the payment gateway is Tekion Pay. This caused confusion and inefficiency, leading to duplicated efforts, inconsistencies, and difficulties in troubleshooting and managing releases, including support training and release notes.

The proposed solution is to implement a centralised payment settings interface within the Payments module in the core system. This interface will consolidate all payment-related configurations, providing a single location for users to configure payment settings and eliminating the need to navigate between Tekion Pay and Cashiering settings.
