Chevy All-in – Credit Tier shopping
Figma
The team has updated the credit tier shopping experience. This feature enables users to select their credit rating range, allowing them to view better finance deals while browsing for a vehicle. It aims to enhance the vehicle purchase experience by providing users with payment and financing options tailored to their financial situation. As a result, users can make more informed decisions when choosing a vehicle.
