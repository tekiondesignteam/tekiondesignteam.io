TAP - Tasks Functionality in Feature Tracker
Figma Prototype
Feature Request has a feature status which is the primary way to monitor the progress of features from Open to LIVE. It offers a high-level overview of what phase each feature is in.
Now, to delve deeper into the process, we're introducing "Tasks" that seamlessly integrate with feature requests. These tasks serve as checkpoints, ensuring that all necessary steps are completed before a feature can be planned or launched.
These can include creating product briefs, finalizing designs, drafting release notes, and preparing marketing collateral. Given that each task often falls under the responsibility of different team members, they're typically executed concurrently, maximizing efficiency throughout the development lifecycle.

TAP - Support Portal - Problem Ticket Management
Figma Prototype
Currently, there are many duplicate or multiple tickets created in different brands
for the same issue which is leading to less productivity of the support team with multiple agents working on the same issue.
In order to solve it, we have introduced a new feature called problem ticket which is a group of all similar tickets and could be linked under one problem ticket as incidents.
This allows the agents to resolve multiple tickets in one session saving a lot of engineering and support bandwidth automatically increasing the productivity of the team.
