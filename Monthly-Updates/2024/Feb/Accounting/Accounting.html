Automated Write-Offs
Figma Link
Car dealerships often encounter persistent small balances post-warranty claims, even after reimbursement. Our new feature allows users to effortlessly address these through a single write-off transaction, automatically generating multiple entries and ensuring accurate financial records. This enhancement streamlines the process, saving valuable time and effort while maintaining financial accuracy.
