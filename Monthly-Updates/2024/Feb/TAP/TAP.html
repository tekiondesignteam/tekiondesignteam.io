Cashiering Refund Approval Flow
Figma Prototype
The solution involves implementing a two-tier approval flow for refunds in the cashiering module. It enforces role-based routing, considering refund amount, department affiliation, and payment mode. Users have the flexibility to define unique roles for different combinations of these parameters, ensuring a tailored and efficient approval process.
