CarBravo – Credit Tier Shopping
Figma File
The CarBravo Platform now includes a new credit tier shopping feature that helps users find better finance deals while browsing for a vehicle. Users can choose their credit rating range to view payment and rate financing options that are tailored to their financial situation. This feature aims to enhance the user experience by providing more transparency and information, allowing users to make a more informed decision when selecting a vehicle.
