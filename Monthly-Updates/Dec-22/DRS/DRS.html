DRS - December Design Updates
 
 
Multi Lender Support
Figma File
 
 
This feature allows the user to choose the type of payment they want to make. Such as finance cash or lease. Multi-lender is applicable only in finance and lease (payment type), where the user can select the lender, i.e., the bank that gives the loan to the customer. Users can select the monthly amount, duration, apr, and lender. The multi-lender can also compare how much extra or less they will pay to the other options in the monthly payment.
 
Sayantani Sarkar
Product Designer
 
 
 
E-Sign Rules (ZCS)
Figma File
 
 
Lenders have some specific rules that are required to be implemented by DMS during the signing ceremony. These rules comprise of order in which forms can be signed and submitted. These rules will be based on the aggregators, and specific directions will follow the order in which the customer (buyer/co-buyer) and the dealer can sign. It will also set expiry rules for documents with a time limit for signing.
 
Raveesh Balakrishnan
Lead Product Designer
 
 
 
Accessories - UX improvement
Figma File
 
 
The accessories should look exciting and more descriptive to increase vehicle accessory sales. We need to make an impression in buyers’ minds that it will give their vehicle a premium and better look than it would without them. In this design update, we have highlighted the accessory’s image and description. Below the accessory name, we have added a line stating the primary use of that particular accessory. The users can also switch between accessories and compare the pricing and different accessories upfront.
 
Harshita Dhirendrakumar Vyas
Associate Product Designer
