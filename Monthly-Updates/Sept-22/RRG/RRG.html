Service: Appointment Flow - Desktop
 
Jayrajsinh R Jadeja | Figma Prototype
 
 
In order to accommodate the needs of service advisors at Renault Retail Group Dealerships, we are introducing an updated Appointment flow. This will allow service advisors to add relevant jobs, parts, fees and assign a technician before the date of the appointment. Parts: Stock Valuation and Report
 
Vidhisha Kedia | Figma Prototype
 
 
Implement an advanced stock valuation calculation method that takes into account the history of stock entries with prices.

Inventory valuation is a calculation of the value of the parts in the inventory at the end of a particular accounting period. Stock valuation part groupments will be used for calculation. The definition of part groupment is described in PRD part groupment Stock valuation calculation will be used to put correct values in accounting postings linked to valuation at the end of every month.
 
 
 
Parts: Web Picklist Experience

As a warehouse employee, I need to confirm picked quantities after the generation of a picklist. Only after this confirmation, the picked parts will be removed from the stock, and property transfer will be done. Initial picklist generation will not have any impact on parts state.
 
 
 
Core: Employee Onboarding
 
Niranjan Gupta | Figma Prototype
 
 
About
The existing employee onboarding screen required a few additions and removal of fields according to RRGs requirements. In this project, we were required to adapt to RRGs requirements. Keeping in mind that it doesnt require major changes in the existing system.
Challenges
Understand how employee fields are used in other modules in the ARC and identify the rights fields coming from ADP (HRIS Integration) to map to the employee fields in the ARC.
Learning
Learned about how a tenant is structured and why? How employee records are consumed in other modules to provide useful functionalities.
 
 
 
Core: Automatic Role & Rights Assignment
 
Niranjan Gupta | Figma Prototype
 
 
About
We needed to create a mechanism for automatic role & rights assignment. Based on combinations of fields from ADP (HRIS integration) for RRG. So that during onboarding, we dont have to do it manually for every employee record created.
Challenges
Understanding how RRG assigns roles and permissions to an employee in a dealership? Identifying fields in employee records that can be used to create a role assignment matrix and making it scalable so that it scales for dealerships in other countries.
Learning
How the structure of a tenant influence the roles and rights definition for an employee. How are employees assigned roles based on their working location in a dealership?
 
 
 
Core: GDPR Configurations for European dealerships
 
Sougata Nair | Figma Prototype
 
 
We have introduced features which lets our dealerships manage their customers' data better and be compliant with European Data Privacy Regulations.

This features includes a comprehensive dashboard to manage customers consent to use their personal data and their requests for exporting, correction, deletion of data and more. Dealerships users can also configure different aspects of the feature to be compliant with GDPR norms in any European country. The feature, although being piloted for RRG, will be scalable towards any country in Europe going forward.
