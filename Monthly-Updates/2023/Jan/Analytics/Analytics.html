Analytics - January Design Updates
 
 
Parts Dashboard (Wholesale)
Figma Prototype
 
 
For the average dealer, the amount of parts sold via wholesale (to other dealerships, repair shops and body shops) is as much or more than the value of parts sold to the dealership’s own service department.

So, designed wholesale tab for parts dashboard with an interactive widgets to monitor the following data:

- Returning a higher percentage of the parts purchased
- Consistently late in making payments for the parts they’ve purchased
- Ordering less than they used to in the past

Inventory Dashboard 2.0
Figma Prototype
 
 
Inventory dashboard underwent design changes with a similar look & feel to sales & service dashboard. Few updates:
- KPIs section with thumbnail of the trend-line
- Stock Analysis - allows users to compare the profitability of a vehicle with the current stock status
- New and Used inventory - users look at the Hot/Cold status of cars and highlight the important vehicles
- Age Analysis - helps users to analyze the age vs GL balance of their inventory
