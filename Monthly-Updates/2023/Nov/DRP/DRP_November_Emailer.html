Cadillac – Compare Vehicles Flow
Figma File
The team has updated the Vehicle Compare feature, incorporating VSR and VDP. This enables users to compare up to three vehicles when accessing our platform through web browsers and tablets. On mobile devices, users can compare two vehicles. This functionality ensures a comprehensive experience, allowing individuals to make well-informed decisions by evaluating multiple vehicles across various specifications effortlessly.

Cadillac - Trade-in Upload Images
Figma File
The team has updated the trade-in process to allow users to upload images of their vehicles from multiple angles, including front, back, and sides, interiors, and highlighting any damages. By enabling users to share these detailed images, we aim to empower dealers to generate a more precise and informed trade-in estimated value, ensuring a transparent and accurate assessment of the vehicle's condition.
