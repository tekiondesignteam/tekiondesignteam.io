TAP CRM - SMs View for Dealer Products Status
Figma Prototype
Problem Statement:
When any one / small product from the list of products availed by the dealer is marked as live, then the entire dealer got marked as live currently. So it’s very confusing for the dealers to see, which product went live that made the dealer status itself to go live.
Solution:
Added an additional “Products” tab in Dealers section in TAP CRM, which provides product level status for a specific dealership to Solution Managers
