Advance Search - Vendor Management
Figma Link
There is a range of data associated with vendors, and finding the right one with the necessary information can be quite challenging. Therefore, we have introduced advanced search capabilities that allow users to refine search parameters, thereby narrowing down the results. This empowers dealer users to quickly pinpoint the exact vendor they are looking for and reduces the time and ambiguity involved in the search process.

Figma Link

Duplicate Prevention Rule - UX Improvements
Figma Link
With this update we are simplifying the creation of Duplicate Prevention Rules. These rules allow us to automatically identify and prevent the creation of duplicate customer records by matching specific field values against predefined criteria. This enhancement significantly enhances dealerships data integrity and ensures that they can maintain a single, reliable source of truth for every customer.

Merge Duplicate Customers - UX Improvements
Figma Link
In our Merge process, we are introducing a feature that allows DP Admins to permanently dismiss data clusters that are suggested by the ML system but are not deemed relevant. This empowers our DP Admins to make more informed decisions. Additionally, we've improved the display of open documents associated with a customer during the merging process. This enhancement provides our DP Users with better visibility of relevant documents, thereby helping them maintain accurate customer records.

Figma Link
We've implemented customisation options for the retention period of customer’s (PII) data, aligning with data privacy standards similar to GDPR.
