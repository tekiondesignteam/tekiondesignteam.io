Accessories Rules
Figma File
Selection of certain accessories necessitates the addition or removal of other accessories. In our update, when a user selects such an accessory, we display a bottom sheet showing the required or removable accessories and their associated price changes for the total payment.
Harshita Vyas
Associate Product Designer
Custom Order - ACH payment flow
Figma File
Upon finalizing the customization of their vehicle, users are presented with the convenience of making their payment on the summary page, offering two options: through a credit card or via a bank account, with the bank account transactions utilizing the ACH method. In order to ensure a smooth and secure payment experience, we have proactively addressed and accounted for a variety of scenarios that users may come across during the payment process, providing a robust and user-friendly payment solution.
Raveesh Balakrishnan
Lead Product Designer
Micro Interactions
Figma File
In our recent update, we've improved the user experience with micro-interactions. For example, when ordering a custom vehicle, there's a short delay before reaching the payment screen; we have added animations to keep the user informed and engaged. In the 'My Orders' section, we have added subtle ripples near the kebab menu to indicate new documents ready for download.
