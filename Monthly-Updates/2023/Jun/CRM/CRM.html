CRM - June 2023 Design Updates
 
 
AI Capabilities in CRM!
Figma Prototype
 
 
Introducing everyone’s favourite AI capabilities to our CRM! Starting with two features—1. Smart Draft: users can take advantage of quick responses to reply or prompt the AI to generate text/email content. The AI Modal leverages the complete history of interactions with the customer to generate personalised and contextually relevant communication.
2. Lead Snapshot: A quick catch-up on the lead activity so far! An easy to glance snapshot of the lead journey with all the prominent milestones and valuable insights. No more jumping around various tabs to arrive on the same page.
 
Vanitha Sushma
Lead Product Designer
 
 
 
Auto de-duplication of CRM contacts
Figma Prototype
 
 
Introducing Auto De-Duplication for CRM Contacts. Users can exclude and modify specific contacts before monthly Auto-merge runs, ensuring clean records and reducing errors. Merge History provides a detailed record of past merging activities, allowing users to track and review the merged contacts.
 
Ogriwala Mrunali Pareshkumar
Associate Product Designer
 
 
 
Campaign Updates
Figma Prototype
 
 
The campaign creation flow now includes Standard Segment Recommendations, enabling users to quickly start campaigns by offering predefined target audience groups that are commonly used across the industry.
Additionally, we have enhanced the placement of the exclude conditions, allowing users to conveniently exclude specific customers while setting up their target audience.
