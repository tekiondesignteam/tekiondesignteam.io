DRS - June Design Updates
 
 
Inchcape - Credit Application Flow
Figma Prototype
 
 
We have developed Inchcape, an in-store customer experience that integrates with our CRM system to gather customer information. Customers can enhance their shopping experience through this platform by adding accessories and selecting a protection plan. Additionally, we have implemented a Funding feature that allows users to compare prices and make informed decisions. Our platform also facilitates document uploads and lets users sign digitally, eliminating the need for paper and promoting contactless sales at the dealership. We have accounted for various scenarios to ensure a positive user experience, including error handling and other edge cases. In future updates, we aim to further enhance the platform's intuitiveness.

Inchcape - Reservation Flow
Figma Prototype
 
 
In the last month, we have devoted our efforts to developing the Inchcape Reservation flow, which enables customers to reserve vehicles at any process stage. Utilizing our proprietary Tekion Pay app, customers can conveniently make reservations. Moreover, our system accommodates the flexibility for customers to switch vehicles even after making a reservation. A nominal fee of ₤99.00 is typically applied for each vehicle reservation. To ensure a comprehensive user experience, we have diligently considered various edge cases, including error handling, waiting scenarios, successful reservations, and more.

DRS - Enhanced Unlock Pricing Flow
Figma Prototype
 
 
In this update, we've added a placeholder to display the MSRP and Selling price upfront on the VSR card and VDP page. Dealers have the option to show both or choose one. Additionally, dealers can lock specific prices to generate leads while keeping MSRP and selling prices visible. Clicking the Unlock Pricing CTA opens a side sheet for lead generation. We've implemented various combinations of unlock pricing to optimize lead generation for dealers.
