(Inchcape) Manual Receipts
for Vendor Special Order for BA
Prototype Link
 
 
What?
To Introduce Manual Receipt - Special Order for an RO/SO/ SOR
Why?
  • Bravo Auto users orders parts over the phone (or through OEM website). In many cases, the part number is not known. They share part description with the vendor and the RO reference (in some cases vehicle lic). PO is created after the part is created. In many cases part is added in the RO after PO is received,
How?
  1. By enabling users to create a manual vendor special order for multiple ROs,
  2. When the parts are added in the respective repair orders after the special order is created & received. Part should be put on RO hold
  3. Since the Order can have multiple parts from various SO/SOR and ROs, making this flow a complex, It is also equally important to show the waiting screen along with possible error scenarios.

Record report snapshot - before and after physical physical inventory
Figma Design Link
 
 
Where?
  • Physical Inventory → Setup
What?
  • Record report snapshot before starting the inventory and after closing the inventory.
Why?
  • The reports and amounts shown in the Setup screen are live and change in real-time. We advise users to download these reports before starting the physical inventory which serves as a starting reference point to compare inventory health after the physical audit is complete.
  • If users do not download this before starting the inventory, they do not have a way to look at the initial figures/reports.
