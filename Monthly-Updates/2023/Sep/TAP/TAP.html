Time Scheduler – is a solution designed to streamline and enhance the way your organisation tracks, manages, and optimises time and attendance of ISM’s and PSMs.

Key Features:
  1. Effortless Time Tracking: ISM’s can record their work hours accurately subjective to each dealer and type of work. Also, whether they're working in-office or remotely.Flexible Entry Options: Flexible entry ensures that time tracking suits various work styles like training, implementation etc.Category and Dealer Allocation: Users can assign specific categories and dealers with clear time allocation. TAP IOT - LPR Reader Dashboard
Figma Prototype
 
 
What is LPR?
LPR stands for License Plate Recognition. Decoding the license plate number from a high-resolution photo of vehicles on premises with surveillance capability.

Why LPR?
  • Customers are hesitant to use RFID tags on the vehicles, also these tags often get damaged due to external factors. (During rain or hot sunny days the tag might get damaged or missed).
  • With the help of LPR cameras, we can augment the RFID solution and capture the vehicle details.
  • The data captured will be used further in Welcome displays and Appointment screen.

Product Requirement -
  • Support team need to manage all the LPR cameras provisioned to dealerships.
  • Through this dashboard they can - See the Camera details with respect to each dealerships
  • Add / Modify the camera details
  • View the last vehicle scans of each camera with all the relevant records
