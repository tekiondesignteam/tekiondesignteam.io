In the VSR, users can place a custom order for a new vehicle or contact the dealer directly to inquire if a vehicle is unavailable or not found. Vehicle Hold & Release
Figma File
This functionality offers users an insight into a vehicle they are contemplating buying. In the VSR card, users will encounter a tag indicating 'purchase pending,' In the VDP, we present a comprehensive page featuring actionable prompts (CTAs) for custom order placement and future availability notifications. Within the VDP, we also display vehicle recommendations that closely align with their preferences. Should a user wish to initiate a custom order, we provide them with step-by-step guidance through the custom order process. Alternatively, selecting 'Notify me' triggers the appearance of a lead form, where users can input basic details and submit them to receive notifications about the vehicle's potential availability for future purchases.

Acura ZDX - VDP : Select Vehicle Charger Type
Figma File
For ZDX vehicles, we have introduced a feature that allows users to choose an electric vehicle charger. Three charger options are presented, each accompanied by its description. Users can also compare these chargers based on criteria such as overnight charge yield, cord length, safety features, weather resistance, and more, enabling them to make an informed decision.

Acura ZDX - Reschedule Appointment
Figma File
To accommodate users' needs, we offer the option to reschedule their appointments. This might be required for various reasons, including scheduling conflicts and unexpected events. Our feature empowers users to choose a different, more convenient appointment date and time slot. While we allow users to change the date, time, and appointment type, we do not support modifications involving the vehicle model or dealership selection.
