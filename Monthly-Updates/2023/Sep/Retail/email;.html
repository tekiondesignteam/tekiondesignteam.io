Paytek Disputes
Figma Prototype
A dispute (also known as a chargeback) occurs when a customer questions the dealership about their payment.

Problem Statement:
  • Disputes require prompt responses to avoid financial loss.
  • Response time for critical disputes is unclear and mixed with others.
  • Lack of clarity on necessary evidence, leading to confusion.

Solution:
  • Introducing a ‘needs response’ tab for critical disputes.
  • Displaying pending response time prominently.
  • Offering recommendations on relevant evidence and dispute resolution best practices.

IoT - Mobile Design Phase 1
Figma Prototype
An existing application for connected displays currently exists. The objective is to overhaul and adapt it for mobile devices, as this will enhance dealers convenience and usability on mobile platforms.

Problem Statement:
1. Users are presently obligated to manually toggle display power on and off, as well as manually inspect display status from a desktop interface.
2. Users are currently compelled to manually edit the template from a desktop environment, which poses inconvenience.
3. Incorporating personal pictures from our devices into the template proves challenging due to extended processing times.

Solutions:
1. Users can seamlessly manage display status using their mobile devices, offering greater control and convenience.
2. Users can now effortlessly modify templates via mobile devices.
