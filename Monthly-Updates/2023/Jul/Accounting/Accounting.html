Inchcape: Advanced JE Templates
Figma Prototype
Inchcape faces challenges in creating journal entries with correct combinations of Departments and relevant GL accounts, leading to difficulties in tracking accounting transactions accurately.
To address this, we empower dealer partners with customised templates and robust rules, preventing posting errors and ensuring streamlined accounting transactions. This enhances efficiency and financial control for Inchcape.

AP - Unallocated Payments
Figma Link
Tekion introduces the Unallocated Payments Management feature to handle payments made to suppliers/vendors without known underlying transactions. Dealer users can record these payments at receipt and later close invoices from suppliers/vendors using them.
This feature distinguishes between credit invoices (likely refunds) and payments, ensuring accurate 1099 reporting and streamlined accounting processes.It enables dealer partners to take control of unallocated payments with Tekion ARC.
