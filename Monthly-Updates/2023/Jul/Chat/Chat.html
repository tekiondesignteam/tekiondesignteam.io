Vehicle Delivery & Test Drive Enhancements
Figma Prototype
 
 
Introducing new enhancements on Advance Test-drive and Vehicle Delivery, they now support multiple assignee selections from both Individual Employees and Teams, providing users with flexibility when scheduling appointments. Furthermore, we've made improvements to the appointment slot selection experience, ensuring it's now more intuitive and straightforward. With our new feature, users can effortlessly drag the slot directly on the calendar, adjusting the appointment time without the need for any typing or manual selection.

Personalised Video Experience
Figma Prototype
 
 
In the market where price difference is not a factor anymore to choose one dealership over another, it's the willingness and personalised experience that brings customers at the door. We've upgraded our video sharing experience, enabling users to effortlessly send customised videos via email or text right from the Lead details page. Moreover, new capabilities such as 'Trim', 'Crop', 'Upload cover', 'Picture On Picture', and 'Notes' have been added, enabling users to edit and tailor their videos before sending them to customers.
