Tekion Pay - Donation Option
Figma Prototype
 
 
Dealerships will now have the option to create and include a donation button for their end customers. This means that when customers make their payments, they will have the choice to add a small additional amount as a donation to a cause they wish to support. It’s a convenient way for customers to contribute to charitable initiatives while completing their transactions.

Support Connect
Figma Prototype
 
 
Support Connect allows support agents to chat, and monitor multiple users at the same place. This new enhancement has been focused on solving the pain point of needing to close on chat in order to access the next one in the queue. Also, with the old design, it was difficult for users to know any incoming chat. We have also added dealer information, live sentiments, module, chat waiting time, Chat started time, and last activity to help agents analyse and access chats accordingly.

Twilio Integration In TAP CRM
Figma Prototype
 
 
Currently, Managers are not able to track the real-time call activities done by their SM’s (40 users) which includes getting the no of incoming/outgoing calls, call recordings, and frequency of missed calls. Later this can be extended to IMs and PSMs. With the integration of Twilio cloud telephony in CRM by seamlessly Integrating Twilio’s advanced call tracking features will enhance our CRM capabilities. This Integration will empower Managers to effectively track and manage customer interactions, improve call quality, automate activity creation, and gain valuable insights through call-related KPI analysis
