Invoice Sequence Settings
Figma Link
We have updated the design for the invoice sequence to capture more advanced use cases. With these latest updates, the dealership can now be configured to have a unique invoice sequence per department group. Additionally, we have added an option to reset the invoice numbers each calendar year. These changes will greatly reduce the ambiguity of the invoices and increase accounting efficiency.

Audit Log - Roles & Permissions
Figma Link
The Roles and Permissions audit log required a redesign as it was previously just a timestamped dump of fields. We have now updated the log's design following the latest audit log guidelines across the platform. The new design captures all events related to role and permission management, including creations, updates, and approvals. This redesign makes the information more meaningful, easier to consume, and understand.

Honda CPRA
Figma Link
To ensure CPRA compliance for Honda, we have made significant improvements to the CPRA module. Additionally, we have expanded the request types for Honda to accommodate automation-related requests and have implemented measures to limit the usage of sensitive information on ARC.

Internal Chat - Delete Message and Read Receipts
Figma Prototype
  1. Delete Message: User should be able to delete the incoming and outgoing customer chats (This will be backed by a permission) and internal chats.
  2. Read Receipts:
  • In 1:1 chat, the sender wants to know if his message has been read by the recipient as this facilitates smoother and more efficient communication.
  • In channels, the read receipt feature ensures that critical announcements or
tasks assigned have been seen by all members.

Internal Chat - Recurring Out of Office Schedule
Figma Prototype
The aim is to allow users to schedule recurring Out of Office (OOO) settings. Currently, users need to set their OOO status each time they are away or on a leave. This feature will enable users to set their OOO status once, and have it apply on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.
