CRM - May 2023 Design Updates
 
 
Email Revamp
Figma Prototype
 
 
The current email experience posed multiple usability concerns. To address these problems, We revamped the email experience to mimic a standard email client with Inbox, drafts and sent. We’ve also introduced subject-wise threading, email tracking, contact management, and automated workflows. This not only resolves the reported issues, but also enables familiar and effective email experience.
 
Tushar Gupta
Product Designer
 
 
 
Lead Qualification - Inchcape
Figma Prototype
 
 
Inchcape has a process to qualify leads where a salesperson collects important customer information like ‘Purpose of purchase’, ’vehicle usage’ etc. and offers unbiased advice considering customer’s preferences during the sales process. To cater to that we have implemented the customisable lead qualification form which is easily accessible from the edit lead page.
 
Hal Habeel
Senior Product Designer
 
 
 
Quick Quote 2.0
Figma Prototype
 
 
Quick Quote is used to send a quote to customers via email without creating a deal. Currently, Quick Quote is similar to an EMI calculator with a limited number of payment options, which forces users to create a deal and then share the Deal sheet. However, with this new update users can now generate a quote without creating a deal and send an embedded or PDF version. Additionally, we have added the same capabilities as the Deal sheet, including the ability to add up to 5 payment type comparisons and customise the PDF field options.
 
Deenadayalan Chandrasekar
Associate Product Designer
