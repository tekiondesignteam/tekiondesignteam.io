Enhancement to Lost Sales
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
The counterperson looses sale to a customer due to insufficient inventory and high prices. Users need a way to mark it in the system & a report that gives them part level insights to prevent this from happening.
Why?
Today what we have in the system is not as robust as is in the competitors due to which we have got some considerable amount of feedback from the dealerships.
How?
With this enhancement users will be able to
  1. Mark lost sales in SO & RO with specific reasons (part line level & bulk level)
  2. See the lost sale report at part level

[Inchcape] Introducing Imprest Stock
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
Some dealerships will carry stock that has been supplied by a supplier, where the supplier will only send an invoice for the tyres once the Dealership sells the tyre. In the UK this is known as Imprest Stock, in the US this is known as Consignments.
Why?
Imprest stock helps to speed up customer repair times by having the stock on hand without having to wait for deliveries.
How?
  1. Users will be able to mark a part to have an imprest stock or not in P&I
  2. If a part is marked to have an imprest stock, this information will be accessible from SO & RO too
  3. A report will be available for imprest stock

Credit and sale on the same document
Figma Design Link
 
 
What & Why?
The customer might want to return & exchange it for a new part on the same invoice on which the original sale might had happened. Today, the user returns on one invoice and sells the new part in exchange in another invoice, which results in too many cashier transactions causing inconvenience to the end customer. This is a highly demanded feature by dealerships.
How?
  1. Enabling the counterperson to reopen a closed invoice and allowing them to do a new sale with reference to an existing CM
  2. Enabling the counterperson to do an unreferenced credit and sale on the SO
  3. In case of the part was sold on multiple invoices in an SO, the users can choose against which invoice do they want to do an exchange

Apply PO discount on a document level
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
  • The dealerships should be able to apply the discount coupons configured in the coupon management on a PO document level.
Why?
  • In the current system, the dealer partners can apply discounts on a part-line level. But most of the DP prefers to apply the discounts on a document level which will get reflected in the invoicing and accounting.
How?
  • Now dealerships can add a PO discount by clicking a button and have the flexibility to choose the discount coupons configured in the coupon management module. The discounts will be limited to one at a time. The same discount will be reflected in the invoice PDFs.

PO Creation Approval Workflow
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
  • Approval workflow management is a Business Process Management (BPM) tool to put in place checks and balances for various actions performed by company employees.
Why?
  • Approval management gives company managers and executives an efficient way to monitor and approve/reject unnecessary or unwanted actions prior to execution.
How?
  • Upon the creation of the PO, if the PO amount exceeds the defined value, an approval request will be sent to the concerned person. Now the persona(Manager) will have to approve/reject the request based on the submitted PO requirements.

Bulk action on Supersession for P&I
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?

Physical Inventory Reconciliation Revamp
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
The current reconciliation process has multiple challenges thus making the process laborious and inefficient.
Why?
Some of the challenges faced today are:
  • Hidden true variance under the on-hand adjustment log tab
  • Inability to bulk select parts for adjustments
  • Lack of visibility for write-in parts
  • Inflated variance for parts with multiple bins
  • Need for a part-level variance view and adjustments for parts on hold outside of the module
  • Inability to filter and sort parts based on cost and total variance amount
  • Absence of printable versions for the final variance and adjustment amount
How?
There will be a new provision made to the users to be able to reopen the physical inventory so as to enter counts again, this would take the user from the Reconciliation phase back to the Counting phase, where he can update the counts again.This Recount ability would be provided to the user two times.
