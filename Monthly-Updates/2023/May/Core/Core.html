Report Builder - Cross-sharing of reports from corporate workspace to dealer workspace
Figma Prototype
Currently, we offer users the ability to create reports in the dealer workspace, which are accessible only within that store. Alternatively, users can create reports in the enterprise workspace. With this feature, users will be able to share enterprise reports with dealer users across different stores, or they can create a report in the dealer workspace and share it with other dealers.

Sequence Settings: Invoices
Figma Link
As a part of onboarding dealerships Integration Teams at Tekion needed a way to manage all the sequence numbers of the invoices such that they can reset them after the data has been migrated into Tekion. Specifically, for the invoices we designed the ability to add multiple prefixes and suffixes along with the regular sequencing updates we designed earlier.

Inchcape: Credit Limit Approval
Figma Link
Users who are serving the customers could ask for an increase in Credit Limit. Till now, they could simply edit the Credit Limit themselves. For Inchcape, we will introduce a process where these users will have to request for the Credit Limit upgrade, which in turn would be approved by an Accounting user.

Product Design Lead
Internal App : Permissions
Figma Link
Build an internal app which will enable the user to streamline the process of Permission addition, editing and approval. The application will be used by various departments within the company to manage permissions for different products. The primary users of the application will be engineering managers and product managers. This application should allow users to create new permissions, edit existing permissions, and create new modules for permissions.
Neha Tibrewal
Product Designer
Notification Settings - Enterprise, Dealership and User
Figma Link
Currently, all the notification settings are at the user level and the user has to configure them according to their role and usage. Enterprise and dealership user wanted an ability to configure this at the enterprise and dealership level. So that they can have more control on who gets what notification based on their role.
