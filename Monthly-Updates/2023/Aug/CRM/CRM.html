Campaign Failure Reasons
Figma Prototype
Currently, dealerships can view the overall insights of any campaign. However, at the moment, users can only see the reasons for communication failures at an individual contact level and there is no way to get an aggregate view of all the failed reasons.

To address this issue, we have now introduced the failure reasons at the campaign insights level, providing a holistic view of the total percentage and the reasons why the communications failed. Ensuring an easy consumption of data along with reducing the no. of clicks.
