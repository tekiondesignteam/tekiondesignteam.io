Accounting - August Design Highlights
Journal Entry Error Resolutions
Figma Link
During migrations or setup changes, any adjustment on auto-posted settings leads to a significant increase in erroneous journal entries. As we currently don't highlight these errors, or provide any resolution guide—the manual effort of identifying and fixing these one-by one, leads to user frustration and increased support tickets. With this enhancement, our users will now have an improved error visibility, guidance on resolution, and the vision of overall impact.
Hardik Batra
Senior Product Designer
Tax Settings Enhancements
Figma Link
This enhancement simplifies VAT code determination for both sales and purchase transactions across the application. Dealer partners can effortlessly create new tax codes and categories like VAT or Customs Duty, maintain vital disclosures for each tax code, and manage GL accounts at the tax code level for precise tax postings.
The central tax engine streamlines the targeting of tax codes for sales and purchase transactions, enhancing efficiency. This feature also covers the creation of customer, vendor, product/service tax classifications, and business contexts, ensuring complete control and simplification of tax-related processes.

Inchcape & EU: VAT Reporting
Figma Link
Our VAT reporting feature streamlines the process of reporting VAT collected during specific periods to tax authorities. It allows dealer partners to view taxable amounts and transaction-level VAT details, download reports in Excel, differentiate transactions by VAT type, and easily spot discrepancies in VAT amounts. This comprehensive tool simplifies VAT reporting, ensuring accuracy and compliance while providing valuable transaction insights.
