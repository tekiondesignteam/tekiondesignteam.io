Employee E-Signature
Figma Link
Dealers utilize ARC for payment creation and Real Imaging for check printing. However, the number of payments can fluctuate among dealers, and signing authorities may need to sign anywhere from 1 to X checks in a given month. Consequently, dealers seek the capability to print payments in a "ready to pay" state, with the signing authority's signature printed when the checks are generated.
