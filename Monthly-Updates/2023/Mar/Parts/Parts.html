Fractional Sales
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
Fractional sales in the automotive parts sales refer to the sale of parts or components of a larger purchase quantity, in which unit of measure and selling is different from original Purchase Qty
Why?
There is no fractional sale available in Tekion currently and this is a much needed ask from dealerships irrespective of their region.
How?
By enabling fractional sales, users will be able to sell a fraction of larger qty of a part. in addition,
  1. Enable and disable fractional sale on part level
  2. Bulk action
  3. ability to add multiple Unit of measures
  4. Save different sale templates

Avg Cost in Parts [Inchcape]
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
It's a realistic cost of parts by averaging it out in all documents, reporting, accounting & inventory valuation
Why?
When parts are specially ordered, the cost of part to the dealership is generally more as compared to a normal order. The actual cost of purchase does not reflect in anywhere in the documents or accounting. Due to this gap, dealerships are making less profit on special order part sale
How?
Average cost of parts should be calculated at every entry of stock and should be maintained at inventory level
For parts that are sold from the inventory, average cost of part should be used in all the documents, reporting & accounting
For specially ordered parts, the actual cost of part should flow into the selling document

Automatic Source Code Movements
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
An ability to have the intelligence in the system to move a part from once source code to another when they meet a specific criteria either realtime or at a specific trigger ( say master update)
Why?
Source Code of a part is a critical functionality to drive the parts operations at a DP. Source codes drive a lot of decisioning particularly around default pricing as well as the storage and ordering of parts. Source Codes are also used for various other purposes to group a particular set of parts with a certain characteristics.
How?
Under OEM settings, allowing the users with an option for automatic source code movements option with
  1. Ability to define one rule as a combination of multiple fields/combinational logic
  2. Ability to configure more than one criteria/rules for auto-source code movements
  3. Ability to run this auto movements on demand, factory master update and addition of new part
  4. Ability to prioritise amongst the criteria in the rule engine by simple drag and drop ordering

Label Configurator
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
Providing the users the ability to make custom label templates for different types of labels available in the system
Why?
Currently, the users are limited to choose labels from the existing templates. With every new design they wanted, Tekion was designing it for them. They could not even modify them according to their own needs.
How?
Providing them a label configurator which comes under Template configurator in Parts settings to create custom label templates for themselves. They can
• Create labels of 2 sizes
• Show/ Hide different fields
• Choose between different font sizes for fields
• Reposition the label contents
• Edit / Delete existing labels

CCC Integration
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
An ability to have the intelligence in the system to share ETA to CCC One via DMS Quote request: Quote is an inbound service sent from CCC to DMS dealer for getting the part pricing and availability information of one or more parts.
Quote Response: Is a outbound response to Quote request sent by DMS to CCC which contains the part pricing and availability of one or more parts.
Why?
In the quote response, Dealers need to send the part ETA details via DSM. So we need to give dealer the flexibility to add ETA that can be sent to CCC for: Parts In- Stock, Parts Out-of Stock/ backordered
How?
Under Parts settings, allowing the users with an option for CCC One ETA information Ability to Add ZIP code for respective areas. Ability to configure days for Parts In- Stock and Parts Out-of Stock/ backordered availability in respect to no. of days.

RRG: Invoices & Parts Invoice Reconciliation
Figma Design Link
 
 
What?
Build an internal application to track and take appropriate reconciling actions between an OEM invoice & the receiving details. This feature will be an RRG exclusive feature but looking to expand into other markets.
Why?
In the current system its very difficult to track and reconcile invoices in the system. Even though we are showing the reconciliation info user cannot perform any action. All the actions (claim reporting, credit/claim reconciliation) happens manually outside the system and record it manually in the system.
How?
By using the claim reconciliation app, the dealerships will be able to reconcile the invoice that they received from various sources(Invoice reconciliation, damage parts reporting etc...) against a credit invoice.
