Experience - Tek-overflow
Figma Link
Tek-Overflow is a community platform where developers can ask and answer technical questions related to software development. Subsequently, other users can contribute by offering answers, feedback, and recommendations to resolve the issue. The community also has an upvoting and downvoting feature that enables users to rate the relevance and usefulness of the questions and answers. Additionally, users have the opportunity to create and publish their articles on the platform.

Service Design Team
 
Payers in RO with Splits and Job Level Cost Centres
Figma Link
Currently, users are not able to add multiple splits for Pay Types, multiple insurance and warranty splits on jobs easily. With the new Payers concept in RO, Service Advisors, Cashiers and other users will be able add multiple splits, multiple insurance and warranty splits on jobs. Users will also be able to define splits by percentage, totals, operations, components and dynamic splits.
Alok Sharma
Product designer
 
Mandatory walk-through for Service Menu, Recalls, and Deferred Recommendations during Check In
Figma Link
Currently, the Dealerships are concerned that Service Advisors / BDC is acting as order takers and not ensuring upsell for customers. Dealerships would like to have a way to ensure that SAs are making explicit decisions regarding Service Menu offerings, Recalls and Deferred Recommendations during the check-in process. So we are allowing the dealership a mandatory flow for Service Menus, Recalls and Deferred Recommendations at Checkout Validation during the check-in process by this approach.
