Recently Viewed
Figma Prototype
 
 
Salespeople and BDC people handle multiple leads and would frequently need to revisit those leads as part of their workflow. However, it can be time-consuming to search for a specific contact each time they need to return to it. To address this, we've introduced a "Recently viewed" button on all list pages and dashboards, which allows users to easily access the last 20 contacts they viewed with just one click.
