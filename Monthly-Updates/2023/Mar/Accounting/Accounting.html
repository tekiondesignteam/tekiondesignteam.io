Accounting - March Design Updates
 
Bulk Operation - A New Module
Figma Prototype
Our Dealer partners face errors in the GL Account Mapping which are usually detected during the month-end accounts closure, resulting in a tedious and time-consuming manual process to identify and create adjustment entries.
To tackle this issue, a new module called "Bulk Operation" has been developed, which automates the process of creating adjustment entries for all affected entries. This module not only saves time and effort, but it can also be useful for other bulk fixes in the future and is designed to scale effectively.
Hardik Batra
Product Designer
 
AR/AP Shell Invoices
Figma Prototype
During the initial phase of implementing ARC, there may be issues with some transactions not being transferred from the old system. This can create discrepancies that require a lot of effort to address. To make the transition smoother, one solution is to reconcile the Aging balance and GL account balance using Shell invoices. This will help dealer partners balance out the differences and streamline their AR/AP accounting flows.
Hardik Batra
Product Designer
 
 
Business Card Enhancement
Figma Prototype
This update offers a streamlined solution by enabling dealer partners to link business card transactions directly to vendor invoices. This enhancement simplifies the account payable(AP) process and eliminates unnecessary steps, making the workflow more efficient and user-friendly.
