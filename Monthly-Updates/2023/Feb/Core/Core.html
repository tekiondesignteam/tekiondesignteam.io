RRG: Customer Creation/Modification Workflow
Figma Link
 
 
A workflow is required to create a business customer in compliance with the existing process at dealerships. The process is broadly divided into two steps: data capture and data validation. The data required to create the customer is gathered through user-inputted forms, combined with external integration to fetch the correct data and enable quick validation. In the second step, the data is validated by different stakeholders in the dealership and finally approved for the customer to be created.
 
Niranjan Gupta
Product Designer
