Parts Tax Codes Setup
Figma Design Link
 
 
This is the parts consumption for new tax code implementation. The new tax module will have the creation of tax codes (completed by service and accounting teams) and service and parts modules will consume the new tax code setups. Once tax codes have been created, users will create the tax matrix in parts settings. All locations where taxes are editable in parts will be replaced by a tax code grid.
