Media Library - Mobile
Figma Prototype
 
 
Currently, media in ARC is stored from various touchpoints, such as Repair Orders, and becomes inaccessible elsewhere. Additionally, managing, storing, and browsing these files becomes a significant task for our dealer users when they need to reuse them.
