CRM - April 2023 Design Updates
 
 
Multi Domain
Figma Prototype
 
 
Dealerships that sell multiple brands need different email IDs associated with each brand to communicate with their customers. Currently, this requires setting up separate ARC instances and duplicating the setup of users and emails. To address this, we now support setting up multiple email domains within the CRM, allowing multiple brands to be managed efficiently in a single instance of ARC, without the need for duplication.
 
Deenadayalan Chandrasekar
Associate Product Designer
 
 
 
Concierge Launch from CRM
Figma Prototype
 
 
Currently, the concierge process can only be initiated through deals, meaning salespeople must create a deal navigate to that deal and then start the process. However, we now offer the ability to initiate and track the entire concierge progress directly from CRM, without the need to navigate to Deals.
 
Tushar Gupta
Product Designer
 
 
 
Lead Qualification - Custom Feilds
Figma Prototype
 
 
Inchcape has a lead qualification process that uses a questionnaire to effectively qualify leads. To meet their need for customisation, we have introduced a setup that allows them to create custom questions and modify the content by rearranging, renaming or deleting form fields to suit their specific requirements.
 
Piyush Singhania
Senior Product Designer
