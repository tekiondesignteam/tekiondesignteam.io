Check in Setup: Auto Opcode Addition Exception
Figma Link
When a new RO is created or a check-in is done for a vehicle, some opcodes are automatically added. However, there are some situations where such opcodes should not be added automatically. This update allows users to add exceptions to the auto-add rules established in the Check-In Setup.

Dev platform Design Team
Experience - Reported content (Tek-overflow)
Figma Link
In Tek-Overflow, reported content includes questions, answers, and articles that are reported by users as potentially violating community guidelines. Moderators review the reported content to determine if it does indeed violate community guidelines. If a post is found to be in violation, moderators may take action such as blocking the post or editing its content. The ultimate goal is to maintain a respectful and constructive environment for everyone to learn and collaborate in Tek-Overflow community.

Experience - Approval Centre
Figma Link
An approval center is a feature that allows users to request access to records or data, and enables approvers to review and approve or reject those requests based on established criteria. The approval process typically consists of two to three or more stages, where different approvers may be involved at each stage. Once the request is approved by the approvers, the requesting user gains access to the requested records or data.
