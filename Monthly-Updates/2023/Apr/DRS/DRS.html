DRS: April Design Updates
Signing Platform: Seamlessly Switch Between Remote Sessions and Collaborate with Ease
Figma Link
Our platform for signing documents will enable users to alternate between unassisted remote sessions and sessions with remote assistance. It will display all active users present in a signing session and allow participants to work in the background by reducing the iFrame. The platform will also indicate if a form displayed by the host is not relevant to a participant. When a participant exits a session, they will be directed to a non-assisted screen and can rejoin later.

Sign Multiple Documents at Once with Ease
Figma Link
Our E-Sign platform will enable stakeholders to sign all documents at once using the full iFrame version. However, certain documents may require individual signatures to be selected explicitly. In such cases, we will inform the user and handle them separately. Currently, only Dealer Partners have this privilege, but the dealership can later choose which users can access this feature.
