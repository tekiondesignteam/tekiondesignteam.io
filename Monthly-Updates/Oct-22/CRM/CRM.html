CRM - October Design Updates
 
 
Store Overview
Figma Prototype
 
 
Our current mobile CRM dashboard mainly dispalys operational metrics. With the introduction of the Store Overview widget, customers can now keep track of and get insights of the store’s performance at the summary level. They can now get an overview of Total sales, Total gross that they made and compare it with previous data right from their fingertips.
 
Ogriwala Mrunali Pareshkumar
Associate Product Designer
 
 
 
Campaign Metrics
Figma Prototype
 
 
The current campaign metrics provides limited email-related analytics. Users can now keep track of the overall performance of the campaign thanks to the addition of revenue metrics, additional data related to calls, texts, and emails, and the drilldown capability.
 
Deenadayalan Chandrasekar
Associate Product Designer
 
 
 
Lyft Ride Hailing Capability
Figma Prototype
 
 
Currently, managers can book a ride from a RO for a customer, but doing so requires identifying the RO number, visiting the detail page, and then actually booking the ride. We've introduced the new Lyft booking services where managers can now book a ride with just few simple steps for both customers and employees. And employees can route the ride to a specific Deal or RO.
 
Ogriwala Mrunali Pareshkumar
Associate Product Designer
