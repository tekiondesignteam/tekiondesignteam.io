Parts - October Design Updates
 
 
RRG: Appointment -Pre-RO Workflow
Figma Design Link
 
 
Eliminate the need for a technician to go back and forth to the parts department to retrieve parts. Ensure that the right parts are available for the customer’s vehicle, reduces the amount of time a vehicle spends in the service workshop. Eliminate the need for a customer to wait for parts to be ordered. Ability to upsell parts and services to the customer would improved customer satisfaction.
 
Vidhisha Kedia
Senior Product Designer
 
 
 
RRG: Activity Log
Figma Design Link
 
 
A warehouse manager, would be able to follow and identify all the changes in the warehouse (parts movements, data change). The system will track and memorize each change in data or parts movements. Every record type will have a unique identifier. Possibility to investigate in details what have happened in case on any trouble.
 
Kiran B
Product Designer II
 
 
 
Bin Spot Check
Figma Design Link
 
 
Allows users to run a manageable size of inventory audit to compare system count against actual physical count. This will help users identify any quantity mismatch, and research variance, and take corrective actions in due time.
