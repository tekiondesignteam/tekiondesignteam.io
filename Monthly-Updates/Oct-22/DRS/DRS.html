DRS - October Design Updates
 
 
Vehicle History Filter and Report
Figma File
 
 
With this feature, all the buyers looking for used vehicles could apply a filter and get the cars that had only a single previous owner, had no accidents, or were personal vehicles up-front. The buyers can also view the vehicle’s history from the VDP page and access the full vehicle history report, which CARFAX or AutoCheck will provide.
 
Harshita Dhirendrakumar Vyas
Associate Product Designer
 
 
 
Refined Login/Sign Up experience for Pre-qualification
Figma File
 
 
We have revamped the login/signup experience for pre-qualification flow to bring in a seamless experience for the users which is simple and easy to use to achieve the credit estimates faster. Along with this effort we have planned to introduce the key USP’s for any inline login/signup experience to give more context on why login/signup is required for certain flows.
 
Raveesh Balakrishnan
Product Design Lead
