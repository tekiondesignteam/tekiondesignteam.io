TAP CRM - Industrial Conference campaign (NADA)
Figma Prototype
 
 
A new feature in campaigns which covers specific use cases for NADA from contacts registering for NADA through website to scheduling and allocating demo stations for the demos. It covers the entire journey from when the user shows interest to post demo followup. An overview dashboard will allow stakeholders to look at the progress of how the demo's have been performed based on each demo station and SM's. It's been designed in a scalable way so that the same feature can be utilised even for other conference purposes and not only NADA.

Tekion Wallet - Fund transfer to banks & wallets
Figma Prototype
 
 
Now the Tekion wallet users can transfer funds between different wallets and to external bank accounts. This helps the dealerships to move excessive or unused funds available in the wallet to the bank accounts. Tekion wallet uses stripe to integrate and authenticate the payment to external banks.

Calendar - Meeting Poll Feature
Figma Prototype
 
 
A new feature update for calendar module which allows the host to create voting polls and receive inputs from participants. It also has the capability to schedule calls for external participants for conferences and workshops to manage all participants within and outside the organisation. In addition, we created a smart permission management system that allows the poll admin to change who will be able to view or share the votes.
