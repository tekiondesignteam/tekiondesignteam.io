CORE
 
 
Approval Flow - Derogatory Roles and Rights
Figma Prototype
 
 
Automatic roles assignment doesn’t cover all the use cases. There are scenarios in which the roles and their respective rights differ from what we have defined in automatic rights assignment. So in order to cater to such scenarios we needed to design a flow in which a user can be assigned a derogatory role.
 
Challenges
One of the major challenges was to show the rights comparison between the actual and derogatory roles. And also teach the approvers about the rights so that they can make an informed decision while accepting or rejecting it.
 
Learnings
How roles and their rights are assigned to a user or an employee based on the structure of the dealership.
 
Niranjan Gupta
Senior Product Designer

SERVICE
 
 
Vehicle Group
Figma Prototype
 
 
 
Challenges
RRG has a concept of vehicle rank which is used to define labor hours for opcodes. The customisation currently available in Service Catalog offering (for opcodes and service menus) is based on Make, Model, Year, and Trim. There are use cases and requests from existing dealers to build customisations at a VIN prefix basis.
 
Solution
we allowed users to define vehicle groups at a dealer level and then build overrides using the created groups. Users can select the parameters (Make, Model, Year, Trim, Trim Attributes like Fuel Type, Drive Type etc, VIN Prefix) on which to base a group. In the setups screen (Opcode, Labor Rate Configuration, Service Menu Setups, GLAM etc), user can select an existing vehicle group .
 
Jayraj Jadeja
Product Designer
 
 
 
Fee Management
Figma Prototype
 
 
RRG has 2 types of fees, first one is Waste Management Fee also called as Eco participation fee or ECO Tax and the second one is Warranty Management Fee.
Waste Management fee is the amount applied to invoice for waste/recycle management dealership carries out. This fee is only applied on Repair order for customers that are cash paying, term paying or Insurance. Additionally this fee is only applicable if there is a Mechanical or Body shop related activity on RO.
